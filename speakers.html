Ideas worth Spreading      

Speakers for TEDx-MAIT 2019

We are bringing together speakers from different walks of life under one roof to to give talks that are idea-focused yet diverse, to foster learning, give deep insight and to inspire. Our esteemed speakers are all set to provoke conversations that matter.


Praveen Sharma

Motivational Speaker
   

Read More

Praveen Sharma is an intrapreneur by choice, a motivational speaker, business coach, consultant, active public speaker, a writer and also specializes in strategic partnership and hacking business growth for organization. He is trying to break the stigma around mental health with his initiative 'Better Us'. 'Better Us' is a platform to promote the discussion on mental health and related subjects.

Satyam Mishra

Educationalist
 

Read More

Satyam Mishra hails from Bhagalpur in Bihar and holds an engineering degree from the Manipal Institute of Technology. He believes that teachers are vectors of education and proper education for girls is a topic very close to him. He was invited to teach refugee children in Malala Yousafzai All-Girls School in Lebanon in 2017. Currently, he is a National Consultant for secondary classrooms in Teach for India.

Arunima Sharma

Program Manager At Microsoft
 

Read More

Arunima Sharma is a Program Manager intern at Microsoft AI. She is passionate about uplifting the world with innovative technology and strong empathetic leadership. In 2015, she went to Stanford University for a quarter where she learnt entrepreneurship, Machine Learning and was introduced to product management. In 2018, she introduced and founded Product Managers Club at Columbia University with global expansion aspiration.

Nitesh Kumar Jangir

Co-founder at Coeo Labs
 

Read More

Nitesh Kumar Jangir is an electronics engineer with focus on embedded system design. Being the co-founder at Coeo Labs also an active inventor, 6 patents were filed and granted by him in the field of medical devices and artificial intelligence. He is the creator of Saans - a breathing support device to tackle avoidable deaths of premature babies from respiratory distress syndrome due to a lack of immediate access to complex medical equipment. Due to his hard and innovative work, he marked a place for himself in Forbes 30 under 30, 2019 (India and Asia).

Ashim Bery

Percussionist
 

Read More

Ashim Bery is a percussionist based in New Delhi. He has been practicing and learning the Tabla for the last 17 years. In 2015, he began working on a solo project as a tongue drummer and handpan artist. Through his work, he tries to fuse music with works of fellow musicians, artists, theatre professionals, writers, and poets. Each composition of his has a personal story to tell and likes to incorporate storytelling as a medium within the music.

Mriganka Saxena

Architect and Urban Designer
 

Read More

Mriganka Saxena is an Architect and Urban Designer with over 17 years of experience. She has done her M.Sc. in City Design and Social Science from the London School of Economics. Her leadership and contributions in projects, especially the Yamuna River Project, as well as policies, have transformed city transport and created sustainable habitats. It has rejuvenated water bodies and drains, which, in turn, has helped turn around the lives of many families.

Rachel Cunningham

Chef and Travel Blogger
 

Read More

Rachel Cunningham is a world traveler, and works as a superyacht chef. Owing to her profession, she visits places all over the world, and shares all her experiences on her blog. She has been to 115 countries to date and has been traveling the world for 15 years. Over her years of travel, she has witnessed the sunrise over Machu Picchu, learned to cook with a famous South Korean Monk from Netflix, taught children in Nepal, rode camels in Egypt and danced at a carnival in Trinidad, among many other amazing adventures at exotic locations.

Dr. Tavpritesh Sethi

Assistant Professor at IIIT Delhi
 

Read More

Dr. Tavpritesh Sethi is a physician-scientist and Assistant Professor of Computational Biology at Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology. He has been a visiting faculty member at Stanford University. Dr. Sethi specializes in improving outcomes in neonatal, child and maternal health by bridging medicine and artificial intelligence. His research is focused on the development and deployment of machine-learning-based solutions to enable decisions and policy in pressing healthcare questions.

Paromita Roy

Urban Designer and Architect
 

Read More

Paromita Roy is a reputed architect for contemporary buildings. She was an Urban Designer working in Shanghai but left her lucrative job to have a career dedicated solely to public service. She is also the Deputy General Manager of IRSDC which is the nodal agency for railway stations development in India. At the Home Minister office located in Delhi, she is designated as the knowledge partner working with Amit Shah.

Ayush Jaiswal

Co-Founder of Pesto
 

Read More

Ayush Jaiswal, an overhauling passionate business builder from Varanasi determined to take on the tech industry. He co-founded Pesto with Andrew Linfoot with a mission to turn global software engineering wages into a meritocracy, regardless of where someone is born. Being focussed on upskilling India's 5 million software engineers and pairing them with the worlds top tech companies, he intends to challenge the status quo.
